Welcome

Umentor.JS.ORG® is a non-profit project of an individual. The focus is dedicated to expanding access to computer science, specifically developing JavaScript programming languages ​​in the community and increasing the participation of women and young people who are under-represented in Indonesia.

Our vision is that everyone without age limit in every group of people without a background in understanding computer science has the opportunity to study computer science in this case we are proposing developers of JavaScript programming languages. Umentor.JS.ORG® is supported by generous donors like you.

Thank you notes

Our work is built on extraordinary efforts, by many organizations and individuals who have helped to build, fund and disseminate computer science education. We are grateful to benefit from the tireless assistance of the wider computer science education community, and we thank all partners and individuals who have supported our impact so far.
